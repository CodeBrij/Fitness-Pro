Yoga Exercises

Balasana(Child's Pose)

Kneel, sit back on heels, lower torso, extend arms, relax, breathe deeply, then slowly release.

Read More
Savasana(Corpse Pose)

Lie flat on your back, arms and legs extended, palms facing up, eyes closed, and focus on deep, rhythmic breathing and total relaxation.

Read More
Marjaryasana-Bitilasana(Cat-Cow Pose)

On all fours, inhale as you arch your back (Cow Pose), exhale as you round your spine (Cat Pose), flowing between the two with your breath.

Read More
Uttanasana(Standing Forward Bend)

Stand tall, exhale and fold forward from your hips, keeping legs straight or slightly bent, bringing hands to the floor or grasping opposite elbows, relaxing head and neck, and lengthening spine.

Read More
Paschimottanasana(Seated Forward Bend)

Sit on the floor, legs extended in front, flex feet, inhale to lengthen spine, exhale to fold forward from hips, reaching hands towards feet or holding onto legs, keeping spine straight, and relaxing head and neck towards legs.

Read More
Viparita Karani(Legs-Up-The-Wall Pose)

Lie on your back with legs extended up the wall, forming a right angle, keeping arms relaxed by your sides or in any comfortable position, and focus on deep breathing and relaxation.

Read More
Sukhasana or Lotus Pose(Seated Meditation)

Sit comfortably on the floor, cross your legs, placing each foot beneath the opposite knee, lengthen your spine, place hands on knees or in your lap, and relax shoulders, while maintaining an upright posture for meditation.

Read More
Setu Bandhasana(Bridge Pose)

Lie on your back, bend your knees, place feet flat on the floor hip-width apart, arms alongside body with palms facing down, press feet into the floor, lift hips towards the ceiling, while keeping thighs and feet parallel, and engage core and glutes, holding the pose while breathing steadily.
